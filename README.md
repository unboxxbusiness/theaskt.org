# TheAskt

## Learn AI. Create Opportunities.

TheAskt is an AI career and opportunity platform helping people learn practical AI skills, build real-world experience, and create better opportunities in the modern economy.

We believe AI is creating new opportunities for careers, businesses, freelancing, and entrepreneurship. Our mission is to help individuals stay ahead by learning the skills, tools, and systems that organizations use today.

Through content, training, community, and technology, TheAskt helps people turn AI into career growth and income opportunities.
